About

I am an Assistant Professor at Recep Tayyip Erdoğan University in the Department of Computer Engineering. I have a Ph.D. in Information Systems from Gazi University on Web Application Security using Deep Learning techniques. I have over 14 years of experience in academia and industry. My research interests include web application security, deep learning, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, and explainable AI. I have worked on various projects in collaboration with both academic and industrial partners, focusing on applying AI to enhance security in web applications, healthcare systems, and educational technology.

Research

I work on deep learning for web application security. Specifically, I am interested in developing algorithms that enable robust security for web applications. My research interests are at the intersection of cybersecurity, web security, and machine learning, with a strong focus on explainable artificial intelligence (XAI) and its applications in secure systems.
